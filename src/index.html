Amid wants to help you grow your business.

Lebanese entrepreneurs, just like you, are missing out on revenue every day and we want to change that. Just because you don’t have the time, the patience, or the tools to adapt to modern-day marketing doesn’t mean your product is no good or your service is terrible. We believe in you. As a matter of fact, there is no marketing agency that knows you like we do. Amid, the modern-day marketing agency, was founded by Amid Slim, a self-taught Lebanese entrepreneur who learned marketing through error, trial, and commitment. His idea, in 2013, to consolidate China’s largest shopping sites into one foreign platform was genius but failed to last. Failure left questions only modern-day marketing had the right answers for. Idea was not enough. Risk was not enough. Hustle was not enough. Advertising was also not enough. Amid, the agency, was born 6 years later, in Beirut, with a mission to help other Lebanese-owned businesses last forever.
